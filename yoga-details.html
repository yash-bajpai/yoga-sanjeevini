Authentic Lineage

"Yoga is not an evolution of mind or body, it is a tool to transcend them."

Classical Hatha Yoga at Yoga Sanjeevini is taught as a holistic process. It stems from the Adiyogi, who first transmitted this science 15,000 years ago. We offer these practices exactly as they were intended—without dilution or modification.

In the tradition of Isha, the practices are taught by highly trained instructors who have undergone intensive residential training, ensuring the sanctity and potency of the transmission remains intact.

Why Isha Hatha Yoga?

The Approach

The Benefit

Regular practice results in deep physiological and psychological stability, leading to a state of natural health and effortless joy.
