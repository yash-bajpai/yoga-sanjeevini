Meet Your Instructor

verified Certified Yoga Instructor from Isha Foundation

"Yoga is not just an exercise; it is a way of living your life to the peak of your potential."

Sanjeevini Agrawal is a dedicated practitioner and teacher of Classical Hatha Yoga, trained in the pristine and ancient lineage of the Isha Foundation. Her approach is rooted in the belief that yoga should be practiced in its purest form to truly transform the human system.

Having completed her intensive Hatha Yoga Teacher Training in Coimbatore, India, she brings the weight of a 15,000-year-old tradition to her students, offering tools that go beyond physical flexibility into the realm of mental clarity and spiritual balance.
